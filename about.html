About SFFG

The JMU Science Fiction/Fantasy Guild promotes the science fiction and fantasy genres in all of its forms on JMU campus and in the community.

 

We manage Madicon, an exposition for gaming, dealers, anime, literature, and other fiction genres.

 

SFFG is an organization that is open to all types of people, and are always looking for new and creative ideas.
