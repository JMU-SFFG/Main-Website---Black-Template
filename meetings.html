About SFFG

Meetings are held every week on Monday in Roop idontknowtheroomnumber. Meetings are structured, the schedule being:

  1. Mail
  2. News
  3. Madicon/Feast Announcements if any
  4. Game Plugs
  5. Other

 

Mail and news never take more than ten minutes, so the bulk of the meetings goes to Madicon/Feast announcements, game plugs, and other. Game plugs refers to people plugging their tabletop/roleplaying games, such as Dungeons and Dragons, Shadowrun, Camirilla and the like. Other emcompasses everything else that needs to be plugged but is not game related, such as parties, movie nights and other events.

 

Meetings typically last 35-50 minutes, after which people go to dinner together. Dinner is the social hour of SFFG where we choose a restaurant and go as a group, and is great for new members to get to know people.
